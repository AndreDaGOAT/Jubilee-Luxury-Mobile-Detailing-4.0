JECS Quick Wash is designed to deliver fast, convenient mobile car washes right where you are—at work or in busy parking areas.

We use your location to group nearby requests and route our team efficiently, so you get served quickly with minimal wait time.

Our model is route-driven: we collect accurate location data so operations staff can batch nearby requests and serve customers quickly.
